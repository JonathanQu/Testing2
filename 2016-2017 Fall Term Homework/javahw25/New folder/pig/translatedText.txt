Inway ethay earyay 1878 I ooktay ymay egreeday ofway Octorday ofway Edicinemay ofway ethay Universityway ofway Ondonlay, andway oceededpray otay Etleynay otay ogay oughthray ethay oursecay escribedpray orfay urgeonssay inway ethay armyway. Avinghay ompletedcay ymay udiesstay erethay, I asway ulyday attachedway otay ethay Ifthfay Orthumberlandnay Usiliersfay asway Assistantway Urgeonsay. Ethay egimentray asway ationedstay inway Indiaway atway ethay imetay, andway eforebay I ouldcay oinjay itway, ethay econdsay Afghanway arway adhay okenbray outway. Onway andinglay atway Ombaybay, I earnedlay atthay ymay orpscay adhay advancedway oughthray ethay assespay, andway asway alreadyway eepday inway ethay enemy'sway ountrycay. I ollowedfay, oweverhay, ithway anymay otherway officersway owhay ereway inway ethay amesay ituationsay asway yselfmay, andway ucceededsay inway eachingray Andaharcay inway afetysay, erewhay I oundfay ymay egimentray, andway atway onceway enteredway uponway ymay ewnay utiesday.
